What do you want to know?

Kevin was born and raised in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. He attend and graduated from Piedmont Public Schools. He now attends the University of Oklahoma, where he is pursuing an accelerated 5-year Masters of Science in Management Information Technology. Along with the Masters, he will receive his BBA in MIS with a minor in Nonprofit Organizational Studies.

Kevin will have studied abroad three times by the time he graduates. He has gone to Arezzo, Italy with the President's Leadership Class and participated in an exchange program to Hong Kong. He has experience working in consulting, oil/gas, technology, telecommunications, inbound marketing, retail, and sales. He has many skills, which you can view on the 'Skills' tab. He is highly motivated and high-achieving. He has a 3.97 GPA, while holding leadership positions within campus organizations and in the community. He has a strong passion for tech, consulting and nonprofit sector.
